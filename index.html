    Procedures we shall follow

    During making of your website we will go through the 6 necessary steps to make sure we curate “Quality”.

    Step 1 - brain storming

    We will sit together (on skype if you are not in Gujranwala). Brain storm on problems that your website should / can address. and Make good notes.

    Step 2 - documentation

    Formulate requirement documents. These documents binds software developers with clients. A necessary step to make sure we stick to the project.

    Step 3 - designing

    You pay 50 % advance and I start working on design. I will take a week or so to complete it. As soon as I complete a design, I will ask you for approval.

    Step 4 - 1st prototype

    I will complete first functional prototype of entire project. You will highlight alterations that needs to be addressed. I will get back to work to fix them.

    Step 5 - 2nd prototype

    I deliver 2nd Prototype. This one is final from me. If you still find certain changes in it. I will fix them. But for major changes that takes more then a week to fix, I will charge 50 USD per major fix.

    Step 6 - deployment

    I deploy your project with all features on server and hand you necessary configuration files to give you full ownership of your project.
